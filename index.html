Meet the Band

Meet the legendary members of U2, each bringing their unique flair and unstoppable energy to the world of rock music.

Bono

Vocals

Aside from his captivating voice, Bono can speak fluent Spanish and loves to write poetry in his downtime.

The Edge

Guitar

Known for his iconic sound, The Edge also has a quirky hobby of collecting rare vintage hats from around the globe.

Adam Clayton

Bass Guitar

Adam is not only a bassist but also an avid gardener, specializing in growing exotic orchids.

Larry Mullen Jr.

Drums

Larry, the backbone of U2's rhythm, is a certified yoga instructor and practices daily before performances.
